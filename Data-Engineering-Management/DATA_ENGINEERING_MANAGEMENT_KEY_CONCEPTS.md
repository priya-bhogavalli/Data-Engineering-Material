# Data Engineering Management Key Concepts

## Leadership and Team Management

### Team Structure and Roles
- **Data Engineering Manager**: Technical leadership, team development, strategic planning
- **Senior Data Engineers**: Technical mentorship, architecture decisions, complex problem solving
- **Data Engineers**: Pipeline development, data processing, system maintenance
- **Junior Data Engineers**: Learning and development, supervised project work
- **Data Platform Engineers**: Infrastructure focus, platform development and maintenance
- **DevOps Engineers**: CI/CD, infrastructure automation, monitoring and alerting

### Team Scaling Strategies
- **Hiring Patterns**: Junior vs senior balance, skill diversity, cultural fit
- **Onboarding Process**: Technical training, domain knowledge transfer, mentorship programs
- **Career Development**: Individual growth plans, skill development paths, promotion criteria
- **Knowledge Management**: Documentation standards, knowledge sharing sessions, technical reviews
- **Team Dynamics**: Communication patterns, collaboration tools, conflict resolution

### Technical Leadership
- **Architecture Decisions**: Technology selection, system design, scalability planning
- **Code Quality**: Standards enforcement, review processes, technical debt management
- **Mentorship**: Technical guidance, skill development, career coaching
- **Innovation**: Technology evaluation, proof of concepts, continuous improvement
- **Cross-functional Collaboration**: Working with product, analytics, and business teams
