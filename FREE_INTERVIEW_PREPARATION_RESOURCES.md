## 💡 Study Tips

1. **Start with Core Technologies**: Focus on Python, SQL, and one cloud platform first
2. **Practice Regularly**: Solve at least 2-3 problems daily on coding platforms
3. **Build Projects**: Create end-to-end data pipelines using multiple tools
4. **Join Communities**: Participate in forums and Slack channels
5. **Mock Interviews**: Practice with peers or use free platforms
6. **Stay Updated**: Follow data engineering blogs and newsletters
7. **Document Learning**: Keep notes and create your own cheat sheets

---

**Remember**: The key to success is consistent practice and hands-on experience. Use these free resources to build a strong foundation, but don't forget to apply your knowledge through real projects!

**Good Luck with Your Data Engineering Interviews!** 🚀
